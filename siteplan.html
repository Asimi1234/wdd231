Site Plan: Daily Quote Generator

Site Name

IzyQu QuoteGen

This name captures the purpose of the website — a platform where visitors can discover inspiring, motivational, and thoughtful quotes each day. It’s clear, simple, and reflects the uplifting spirit of the project.

Optional domain availability: dailyquotegen.com

Site Purpose

The Daily Quote Generator website provides a collection of inspirational and motivational quotes. It features a random quote of the day on the homepage and allows visitors to browse quotes by category, search for quotes, and submit their own. The site aims to uplift, motivate, and bring positivity to its visitors through carefully curated words of wisdom.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find today’s inspirational quote?
  • Can I browse quotes by category or author?
  • How can I submit my favorite quote to be featured on the site?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #e76f51 (Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements)
  • Secondary Color: #2a9d8f (Used for headings, borders, and navigation links)
  • Background: #cdcac6 (light background for a clean, minimal look)
  • Text: #333 (Dark gray for body text)

Typography

  • Heading Font: Poppins (Used for page titles and section headings)
  • Body Font: Arial, sans-serif (Used for paragraph and list text)
